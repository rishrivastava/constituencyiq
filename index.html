How IVRS Works - Live Example
AI: Namaste, kya main Ramesh Kumar ji se baat kar sakta hoon? Main Rajesh Sharma ji ki campaign se call kar raha hoon.
Voter: Haan, main Ramesh hoon. Kaun hai aap?
AI: Ramesh ji, main Rajesh Sharma ji ka sahayak hoon. Aap Ward 1, Rajajipuram mein rehte hain - wahan waterlogging ki bahut badi problem hai. Rajesh ji ne is par specifically kaam karna start kar diya hai. 15 tarikh tak drain repair work shuru hoga aapke area mein.
Voter: Haan yaar, har monsoon mein ghar ke saamne paani bhar jaata hai. Kab thik hoga?
AI: Bilkul samajh sakta hoon Ramesh ji. Is baar permanent solution ke liye underground drainage proposal approved ho gaya hai. Aapke ward ke 847 log is se directly benefit karenge. Aap Rajesh ji ko support karenge?
Voter: Dekho, pehle kaam dikhao phir vote milega.
AI: Bilkul sahi baat hai Ramesh ji. Rajesh ji ka exactly yahi kehna hai - kaam se vote, wada se nahi. 15 tarikh ko personally site visit hogi. Ek baar aur mauka dijiye. Dhanyawad!
